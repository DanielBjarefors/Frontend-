Who am I?

I'm an Inspector with the San Francisco Police Department, with the Homicide department, although for disciplinary reasons I occasionally transferred to other less prominent units, such as Personnel or Stakeout. Though proficient at apprehending criminals, my methods are often unconventional; while some claim that I'm prepared to ignore the law and professional and ethical boundaries, regarding them as needless red tape hampering justice, my methods are usually within the law but I take advantage of situations that justify my use of deadly force, sometimes creating those situations my self.
